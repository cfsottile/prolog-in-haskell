prolog-hs

What is it?

is a Haskell module developed by Cristian Sottile for the Functional Programming course of the National University of La Plata. This webapp is Scotty based, and uses the prolog-hs module.

As you may guess from the name, it is a Haskell based Prolog.

How to use it?

You need to provide a *Prolog program*, and a *goal*, and prolog-hs will answer:

* Yeah or Nope, if the goal didn't contain variables;

* the substitutions that satisfies your query, in case there was any, if the goal did contain variables.
